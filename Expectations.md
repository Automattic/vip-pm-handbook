### Communication

> I will communicate as much as possible, because it’s the oxygen of a distributed company. 

Product managers at VIP are responsible for the following reporting requirements:

1. **Internal product demonstrations**: Conduct demos of upcoming releases at VIP Town Halls to build organizational alignment and excitement around new features.

2. **Executive reporting**: Write comprehensive product updates for A8c-wide-view, board materials, shareholder letters, and other required disclosures.

These reporting responsibilities ensure that our work is visible, understood, and properly positioned across the organization.
