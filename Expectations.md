# Expectations

## Team meetings

The Product team meets once a week at 15:00 UTC on Tuesdays. Prioritize this meeting in your schedule. Meetings are a necessary evil. We try to keep meetings to a minimum and focus on documentation and async communication. These meetings have an agenda down to the minute. The goal is not to have a ticking clock, but to quantify what's most important.

### Standard Agenda

- **Banter** (5m): Who doesn't love some banter?
- **Check-in** (5m): Everyone on the team checks in red, yellow, or green.
- **Old Business** (15m): Anything left over from preveous meetings.
- **New Business** (20m): What do we want to talk about today?
- **Actions** (5m): We take time to make sure next actions are captured and assigned.

Each week there is a host assigned, that rotation is managed via Tellspin. The host is responsible for posting [the agenda](https://vipproductp2.wordpress.com/?start=product-meeting) the day ahead of the meeting. They run the meeting according to the above agenda. 

Meetings are recorded starting after check-ins. The recording and lightly edited AI generated notes are posted to the original P2 as a comment by the end of the day.

## Customer contact

PMs should be speaking with at least one customer every week. The formalness of the interaction doesn't matter. If a PMs other responsibilities are preventing them from getting in front of customers that often, they should speak with their team lead to reballance their responsibilities.

## Documentation first

We are an async company and a widely distributed team. When you do something, follow the documentation. When it's out of date update it. If there isn't documentation create it to the extent you can. Even if it isn't perfect. 

This especially includes our conversations with cusotmers. Any customer conversatino should be published or cross-posted to the [Keeping the Feedback Coming](https://keepingthefeedbackcoming.wordpress.com/) P2.
