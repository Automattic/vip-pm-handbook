## Expectations

### Team Meetings

Meetings are a necessary evil. We try to keep meetings to a minimum and focus on documentation and async communication. These meetings have an agenda down to the minute. The goal is not to have a ticking clock, but to quantify what's most important.


#### Standard Agenda

- **Banter** (5m): Who doesn't love some banter?
- **Check-in** (5m): Everyone on the team checks in red, yellow, or green.
- **Old Business** (15m): Anything left over from preveous meetings.
- **New Business** (20m): What do we want to talk about today?
- **Actions** (5m): We take time to make sure next actions are captured and assigned.
