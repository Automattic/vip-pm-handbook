### The PM Loop

- **Discover** – *The PM must understand the jobs to be done, pains, joys, and frustrations for customers and our business, they must also foster this understanding within the team and across the company*
- **Define** – *The PM must work with the team to help generate and define solutions that delight customers and work for our business and theirs*
- **Develop** – *The PM must continuously validate what we have discovered and the solution we have defined and help the team adjust course if required*
- **Deliver** – *The PM must work across our division to ensure that the developed solutions are well marketed, sold, supported, billed, advertised, and used*

🔄 *Once delivered the PM must return to “Discover” to understand if we delivered solutions which delight our customers, and work for our business and theirs…*
